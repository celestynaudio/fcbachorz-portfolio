Demo Reel

My aim is to create quality sound for video games in an efficient and creative manner. I believe that the audio in a game should be immersive, cohesive and thematically appropriate. This demo reel highlights some of my best work so far.

The first clip is a Sound Redesign of one of my favourite games that came out in 2022, Tunic. I used a variety of field recordings, synthesis, and plugins to create all sounds heard here. 'Project Stealth' is a great showcase of my skills in SFX Design, Horizontal Scoring, and Audio Mixing. The last clip, 'Project Convalescnce', displays a wide array of my audio skills, such as atmospheric effects, music composition and integration into Unity Engine and FMOD Studio.

About Me

I am a recent Engineering Graduate from Western University in Ontario, Canada. I have also completed a course with Berklee College of Music for Game Audio Design. My services include SFX Design, music composition and recording, foley design and voiceover work. I am skilled and familar with Middelware software such as FMOD Studio and Wwise Studio, as well as game engines such as Unity or Unreal Engine. Industry standard plugins from Fab-Filter, Waves, Klevgrand and iZoptope are used to heighten any project I am working on.
